New York Style Pizza

Description

No more take out pizza when you make fresh pizza the way that you want. This recipe will teach you everything from the dough to the pie itself.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 6 cups of all purpose flour
  • 2 tbps of olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 tspn of instant yeast
  • 1 tbsp of salt

Sauce

  • Sans Marzano Tomatoes or pureed tomatoes can
  • Olive oil
  • Salt for taste
  • Dried basil
  • Optional: pepper flakes for heat if you want.
  • Low-fat mozzarella cheese
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Toppings: whatever you want!
