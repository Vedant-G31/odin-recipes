Alfredo Fettucine

Description

A rich and delicious creamy sauce that consists of wonderful flavours such as parmesan, garlic, and nutmeg. It is a very easy recipe to make and the ingredients are not hard to find. There is a good chance that you already have them at home. Also, it is a really quick recipe to make if you are looking for something quick to cook. Note: The recipe uses Fettucine, but you can use whichever pasta you want.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 6 ounces grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Optional:1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 8 ounces of fettucine

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water. Add 3 tbsps of salt to the water. When it boils, add the pasta.
  2. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Once melted, add the garlic powder and stir until fully dissolved.
  4. Add the cream cheese and whisk until smooth.
  5. Add milk little at the time, whisk until smooth.
  6. Add the pasta to the saucepan. DO NOT THROW AWAY THE PASTA WATER.
  7. Make sure the heat is at low heat. Add the parmesan, and the pepper.
  8. Add in little amounts of the pasta water for the sauce to emulsify.
  9. Add salt for taste.
  10. Turn off the heat. Once all mixed and you are satisfied with the sauce consistency, then...

Optional:You can add more grated parmesan cheese as garnish. You can do the same for ground nutmeg and pepper.
